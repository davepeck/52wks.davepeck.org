52.WKS :: one minute of original audio every week in 2011.

 

Why 52.WKS?

 

52.WKS isn't about writing great music; it's about improving my quite amateur-level skills as composer, Ableton Live user, and studio engineer. The only way to improve, I suspect, is to flex these muscles on a regular basis. Hence 52.WKS!
